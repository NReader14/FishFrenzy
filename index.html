HOW TO PLAY
OBJECTIVE

Collect all the treats before time runs out. Avoid the shark — it will chase you!

CONTROLS

Arrow Keys or WASD to swim in any direction.

TREATS

🪱 🦐 🦀 🐟 🍤 🍣 🍔 🍕 🍟 🍉 — Collect them all to complete the level. Each treat is worth 10 points (20 during Frenzy). Combo multiplier increases when you collect treats quickly in succession (max x5).

POWER-UPS

Items grouped by rarity. Rarer items spawn less often.

TIPS

Rarer power-ups appear less often and vanish faster — grab them quickly! Watch out for ☠️ Poison and 🧪 Goop. The shark gets faster each level. Build combos for massive scores! Max 3 power-ups on screen at once — limited by a budget that increases each level.

☠ HELL ☠

Somewhere between the waves and the darkness lives something that should not be touched.

If you are unfortunate enough to encounter the 👹 HELL item — do not collect it.

You will survive. You will wish you hadn't.

For 5 seconds, every treat you touch will drain your soul and cost you points. The circles will spin. The screen will bleed red. Then the fire comes.

Those who endure shall hear three words:
“HELL AND BACK.”

🔥 Good luck. You will need it. 🔥
